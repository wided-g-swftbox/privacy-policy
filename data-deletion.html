Data Deletion Instructions

If you would like to request the deletion of any data our app has collected about you, please follow these steps:

  1. Send an email to your-email@example.com with the subject line "Data Deletion Request."
  2. Include the Facebook account email or user ID associated with your data.

We will process your request and delete all associated data within 30 days. You will receive a confirmation email once the deletion is complete.

For any questions, contact us at your-email@example.com.
